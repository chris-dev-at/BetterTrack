Privacy Policy

Version 2.0 — effective 15 July 2026. Deutsche Fassung

Short version: we store what you enter and the minimum needed to run the service. No advertising, no analytics, no sale or sharing of your data. This page is the formal GDPR notice behind that promise — it covers the web app at web.bettertrack.at, the BetterTrack mobile app, and this site.

1. Controller

The controller within the meaning of Art. 4(7) GDPR is the operator of BetterTrack: [OWNER: fill in — full legal name], [OWNER: fill in — street address, postal code, city], Austria. Contact: bettertrack.service@gmail.com. See also the Impressum.

2. What we process

  • Account data — username, e-mail address, password (stored only as a hash), optional second factor and PIN settings.
  • Financial data you enter yourself — portfolios, transactions, holdings, cash movements, watchlists, baskets/conglomerates, alerts and notes. This data is self-entered; it is never pulled from or verified against any bank or broker.
  • Messages — 1:1 friend-chat messages you exchange within the service.
  • Push/device data — if you enable notifications: an FCM push token (Firebase Cloud Messaging, mobile app) or a browser web-push subscription (web app). Created only after your explicit opt-in.
  • Technical minimum — session data, security logs (e.g. failed logins) and e-mail delivery logs for the notifications you enabled.
  • Cookies — two first-party cookies; see the cookie note (§8).

3. Purposes and legal bases (Art. 6 GDPR)

  • Providing the service — account, financial data, chat, sessions: performance of the usage contract, Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR.
  • Push notifications — FCM token / web-push subscription: your consent, Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR; you withdraw it at any time by disabling notifications, which deletes the token.
  • Security and abuse prevention — security logs, rate limiting: legitimate interest in a secure service, Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR.

4. Recipients and processors

We do not sell data, we do not share it for advertising, and we run no ads and no analytics. The only third parties involved are:

  • Google (Firebase Cloud Messaging) — delivers push notifications to the mobile app; sees the push token and message payload metadata. Only used if you enable notifications.
  • Cloudflare — fronts the site for TLS and DDoS protection; traffic passes through it in transit.
  • Market-data providers — receive the ticker symbols being looked up, never your identity or holdings.

Hosting runs on operator-managed servers in Austria — there is no third-party hosting provider with access to your data.

5. International transfers

Google (Firebase Cloud Messaging) and Cloudflare may process data in the United States. These transfers rely on an EU adequacy decision (EU–US Data Privacy Framework) and/or EU Standard Contractual Clauses (Art. 46 GDPR). Your account and financial data stay on servers in Austria.

6. Retention

  • Account, financial data, messages — kept until you delete them or your account; account deletion removes them permanently.
  • Push tokens / web-push subscriptions — deleted when you disable notifications or delete your account.
  • Security and delivery logs — kept for a short, limited window and then rotated out.
  • Backups — encrypted disaster-recovery backups are rotated automatically; deleted data disappears from them as the rotation completes.

7. Your rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • access your data (Art. 15) and rectify it (Art. 16);
  • erasure (Art. 17) — delete your account yourself at any time, in the app under Settings → Account → Delete account or directly at web.bettertrack.at/account/delete; this permanently removes your account and all associated data;
  • restriction of processing (Art. 18) and data portability (Art. 20);
  • object to processing based on legitimate interest (Art. 21);
  • withdraw consent at any time with effect for the future (Art. 7(3));
  • complain to a supervisory authority — in Austria the Datenschutzbehörde (dsb.gv.at), or the authority of your place of residence.

For any request you cannot handle in the app, write to bettertrack.service@gmail.com.

8. Cookies

The service sets two first-party cookies: a session cookie that keeps you signed in, and — only if you use "remember this device" — a remembered-device cookie for PIN quick re-auth. No third-party, advertising or analytics cookies. Details, lifetimes and how to clear them: cookie note.

9. Security

All traffic is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS). Passwords are stored only as salted hashes. Your data is private by default — it is visible only to you and to exactly the people you explicitly share individual items with, and revoking a share closes access immediately.

10. Changes to this policy

If the service changes in a way that affects this policy, we update this page and change the version and date at the top. The current version always lives at bettertrack.at/privacy/.
